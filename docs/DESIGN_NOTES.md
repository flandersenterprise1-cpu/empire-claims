## Idea 1: Brutalist Authority

**Design Movement:** Corporate Brutalism meets Legal Gravitas
**Core Principles:**
- Stark contrast: near-black backgrounds with razor-sharp white typography
- Deliberate asymmetry — content blocks offset to create visual tension
- Structural honesty: borders and lines as design elements, not decoration
- Monumental scale: oversized numerals, large-format headlines

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #0A0A0B (near-black, not pure black — warmer)
- Primary text: #F5F5F0 (warm off-white)
- Accent: #C9A84C (burnished gold — authority, premium)
- Secondary accent: #1A2744 (deep navy for section differentiation)
- Danger/CTA: #B8341B (deep crimson for urgency)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed sections with hard horizontal rule dividers
- Left-anchored text blocks with right-side imagery/numbers
- Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) as structural anchors
- No centered hero — text starts at left 15% from edge

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold horizontal rules above section headings
- Large-format numerals in the background (opacity 5%) as texture
- "EMPIRE" logotype in condensed uppercase serif

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states reveal gold underlines on links
- Buttons have no border-radius — sharp, authoritative rectangles
- CTA buttons use gold fill on dark background

**Animation:**
- Entrance: text slides in from left, images fade from 0 opacity
- Scroll-triggered reveals with 200ms stagger between elements
- No bouncy easing — linear or ease-out only

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, condensed weight) — authority
- Body: DM Sans (geometric sans) — clean readability
- Accent labels: Space Mono (monospace) — technical precision

## Idea 2: Dark Luxury Modernism (CHOSEN)

**Design Movement:** High-End Legal/Financial Dark Luxury
**Core Principles:**
- Depth through layering: multiple shades of near-black create visual hierarchy
- Gold as a precision accent — used sparingly for maximum impact
- Editorial typography: large, confident display text with tight tracking
- Negative space as a power statement — breathing room signals premium

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #0D0F14 (deep charcoal-black)
- Surface: #141720 (slightly lighter for cards/sections)
- Border: #1E2330 (subtle separation)
- Gold accent: #B8963E (warm, muted gold — not garish)
- Gold light: #D4AF5A (lighter gold for hover states)
- Text primary: #F0EDE8 (warm white)
- Text secondary: #8A8FA0 (cool gray)
- CTA blue: #1B4FD8 (deep electric blue for primary actions)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric hero: headline spans 60% left, imagery bleeds right edge
- Alternating left/right content blocks for visual rhythm
- Full-bleed dark sections with contained content columns
- Sticky header with transparent-to-solid scroll behavior

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold left-border accent on key callout blocks
- Diagonal section dividers (clip-path) between major sections
- Subtle noise/grain texture overlay on hero background

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Gold underline sweep on nav links
- CTA buttons: sharp left edge, rounded right (asymmetric radius)
- Cards lift with subtle shadow on hover

**Animation:**
- Framer Motion: fade-up entrance animations on scroll
- Counter animations for statistics/numbers
- Smooth page transitions

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif) — prestige
- Heading: Syne (geometric bold sans) — modern authority
- Body: Inter (clean, readable) — professional
- Labels: Space Grotesk (technical feel)

- Grid-based card layouts for services

**Animation:**
